Why COT?

COT (Coffee or Tea) is a highly versatile token redefined as the RWB (Real World Business) Token, serving as the governance, utility, and liquidity vehicle for a next-generation digital ecosystem that bridges Real World Assets (RWA) and Real World Businesses (RWB). Symbolizing the convergence of traditional and digital paradigms—Web2 and Web3, physical and virtual—“Coffee or Tea” represents how COT empowers traditional businesses to unlock modern tokenization and blockchain-based financial opportunities.
