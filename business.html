For Business

Why Tokenize with Us?

Regulatory Compliance and
Robust Governance

Our platform operates within a highly regulated framework, ensuring your tokenization project adheres to stringent securities laws. This not only fosters investor confidence but also establishes a secure and legally compliant environment, setting your business up for long-term credibility and trust.

Empowered Investor
Engagement

By tokenizing with us, businesses can offer investors tangible rights, such as equity shares, dividends, or other privileges. This feature creates an enhanced value proposition, attracting a wider spectrum of investors, including traditional, institutional, and digitally native audiences.

Unparalleled Transparency
and Accountability

Through blockchain technology, we ensure every transaction is recorded immutably and made accessible for auditing. This transparency builds trust with stakeholders, enabling investors and business partners to trace and verify financial and operational data with ease.

Enhanced Liquidity for
Your Assets

Our platform connects tokenized assets to a global network of secondary markets, significantly increasing their liquidity. This creates new opportunities for businesses to unlock the value of traditionally illiquid assets, like luxury goods or real estate, while providing flexibility to investors.

Global Reach with Local
Expertise

Leveraging cutting-edge technology and a deep understanding of local regulations, we provide businesses with access to global investors while ensuring compliance with regional standards. This dual advantage amplifies your asset's exposure and marketability.

The COT Ecosystem

COT has introduced a digital ecosystem currency tied to tokenized bonds (STO), gaining attention as a key player in digital trading, and is set to launch a stablecoin pegged to the Hong Kong Dollar (HKDT), expected to drive market growth on the D3X platform.
