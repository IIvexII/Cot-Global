The Project?

Welcome to COT_Coffee or Tea

COT (Coffee or Tea) is a highly versatile token redefined as the RWB (Real World Business) Token—designed to serve as the governance, utility, and liquidity vehicle for a next-generation digital ecosystem that bridges Real World Assets (RWA) and Real World Businesses (RWB).

Positioned at the intersection of Web2 enterprises and Web3 infrastructure, COT facilitates the secure tokenization, financing, and fractional trading of tangible and enterprise-backed assets—unlocking and digitizing real-world value for both institutional and retail participants.

All RWB and RWA projects onboarded into the ecosystem undergo independent third-party audits, ensuring transparency, accurate valuation, and investor confidence. Tokenized assets issued through the COT Launchpad are backed by legally verifiable structures, whether equity, revenue share, asset rights, or access models.

By combining asset-backed trust with decentralized financial infrastructure, COT builds a compliant, borderless, and utility-driven Web3 economy—designed to scale and adapt to the rapidly evolving landscape of real-world tokenization.

Our Mission & Vision

  • To democratize ownership of real-world value by enabling secure, transparent, and compliant tokenization of businesses and assets through blockchain.
  • To become the most trusted decentralized infrastructure for Real World Business (RWB) and Real World Asset (RWA) tokenization—enabling scalable financial inclusion and innovation across Asia, the Middle East, and globally.
